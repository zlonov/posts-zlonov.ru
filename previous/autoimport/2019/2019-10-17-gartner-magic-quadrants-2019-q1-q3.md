Сергей Борисов недавно <a href="https://sborisov.blogspot.com/2019/09/blog-post_30.html">поделился</a> в своём блоге ссылками на полные тексты отчётов трёх свежих Квадра(н)тов Гартнера по безопасности:



<ul><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1OEPTVC9&amp;ct=190820&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Network Firewalls</a> (17.09.2019)<br />With firewall providers embedding multiple security features in firewalls and enabling integration and automation capabilities with other security products, firewalls are evolving into network security platforms.</li><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1OHUMHNW&amp;ct=190913&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Web Application Firewalls</a> (17.09.2019)<br />Driven by the adoption of cloud web application and API protection services, the web application firewall market is growing. Network and application security leaders need to evaluate how WAFs can provide improved security that’s easy to consume and manage, while respecting data privacy requirements.</li><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1OCBC1P5&amp;ct=190731&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Endpoint Protection Platforms</a> (20.08.2019)<br />The endpoint protection market is transforming as new approaches challenge the status quo. We evaluated solutions with an emphasis on hardening, detection of advanced and fileless attacks, and response capabilities, favoring cloud-delivered solutions that provide a fusion of products and services.</li><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1OAZDU37&amp;ct=190723&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Security Awareness Computer-Based Training</a> (18.07.2019)<br />People influence security more than technology or policy, and cybercriminals know how to exploit human behaviors. Security and risk management leaders should invest in tools that increase awareness and influence behavior that supports security business objectives through computer-based training.</li></ul>



Добавлю к этому списку ещё несколько Magic Quadrant'ов, вышедших в этом году:



<ul><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1OAD7RCV&amp;ct=190717&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Integrated Risk Management Solutions</a> (15.07.2019)<br />An IRM strategy reduces siloed risk domains and supports dynamic business decision making via risk-data correlations and shared risk processes. Security and risk management leaders should use this Magic Quadrant to identify technology solutions that support this strategy.</li><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1OE2UNB6&amp;ct=190814&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Access Management</a> (12.08.2019)<br />SaaS-delivered access management has become the norm, as has advanced user authentication including MFA. AM vendors are maturing their approaches to session management, contextual and adaptive access, and API protection, which will begin to enable CARTA-aligned access management approaches.</li><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1OC1AO2I&amp;ct=190729&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Unified Endpoint Management Tools</a> (06.08.2019)<br />UEM tools continue to mature as buyers migrate PCs away from legacy client management tools. I&amp;O leaders responsible for mobile and endpoint strategy must evaluate these capabilities along with UEM tools’ ability to perform modern management of PCs and management of mobile devices.</li><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1S52A3SQ&amp;ct=191011&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for Identity Governance and Administration</a> (09.10.2019)<br />IGA is a mature market that faces increasing client demand for cloud-delivered solutions and is splitting between solutions focused on either basic or mature IGA requirements. Security and risk management leaders should scrutinize vendors for compelling roadmaps and a track record of innovation.</li><li><a href="https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-1AN6DWYC&amp;ct=190708&amp;st=sb">Magic Quadrant for IT Risk Management</a> (03.07.2019)<br />Demand for ITRM solutions continues to increase as digital ecosystems evolve. Security and risk management leaders managing cybersecurity initiatives, board risk oversight and digital compliance should use this research to evaluate the opportunities and challenges in automating IT risk workflows.</li></ul>
